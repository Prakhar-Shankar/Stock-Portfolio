About Our Stock Portfolio Manager


Welcome to Stock Loom, your go-to solution for managing your stock portfolio effortlessly. Built with Django and powered by the IEX Cloud API, our platform is designed to simplify your investment tracking experience.

Our Mission

At Stock Loom, we believe in providing a hassle-free platform for users to monitor their stock portfolios with ease. Whether you're a seasoned investor or new to the world of stocks, our goal is to streamline the process of staying informed about your investments.

Key Features


Real-Time Stock Data
Harnessing the power of the IEX Cloud API, we deliver real-time stock data straight to your fingertips. Stay updated on market trends, stock prices, and make informed decisions confidently.

Django Admin Integration
Our integration of the Django admin interface ensures a seamless experience in managing your stocks. Add, update, or remove stocks effortlessly through our user-friendly admin panel.
